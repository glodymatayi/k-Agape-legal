1) Scope

K-agape (“we”, “us”) operates a faith-based matching service. This policy explains how we collect, use, and share personal information when you visit our website and submit an application.

2) Data we collect

  • Contact: name, email, phone (may be verified by SMS).
  • Profile: gender, date of birth, country/city, denomination/church, church attendance, relationship goals, social links, and your faith narrative.
  • Technical: device/browser info, IP-derived region, referrer/UTM parameters; basic analytics.
  • Verification: phone verification status and a transient verification user ID from Firebase Auth.
  • Uploads (optional): photo(s) you choose to provide.
  • Sensitive data: faith-related information you submit. We only process this with your explicit consent for matching purposes.

3) How we use data

  • Review applications and determine eligibility for onboarding.
  • Verify identity (e.g., SMS), prevent abuse, and protect the community (e.g., Firebase App Check).
  • Communicate decisions, onboarding steps, and—if opted in—updates and invitations.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the service; comply with law; enforce policies.

4) Lawful bases (GDPR, if applicable)

  • Consent: faith information; marketing emails (opt-in).
  • Contract: to process your application and provide requested services.
  • Legitimate interests: safety, fraud prevention, service improvement.
  • Legal obligation: to comply with applicable laws and lawful requests.

5) Sharing & processors

We do not sell your personal data. We share information with service providers who help us run the service, including:

  • Google Firebase: Firestore, Storage, Authentication, App Check.
  • SMS delivery via Firebase Auth (Google): for phone verification.
  • Analytics/hosting providers used to operate our website.

Access is limited to authorized staff and processors under appropriate agreements.

6) reCAPTCHA & App Check

We use Google reCAPTCHA and Firebase App Check to protect our forms from abuse. reCAPTCHA is subject to Google’s Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

7) Retention

We retain application data as long as necessary for review, safety, and legal compliance. You may request deletion at any time.

8) Your rights

  • Request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your data.
  • Object to certain processing or withdraw consent (e.g., marketing) at any time.
  • Data portability (where applicable).
  • Lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority (EU/UK residents).

9) Security

We apply administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect your data, including Firebase security rules, least-privilege access, and monitoring. No system is 100% secure.

10) Children

K-agape is for adults 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided data, contact us to remove it.

11) International transfers

Your data may be processed in countries where our processors operate (e.g., the United States). Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

12) Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted with a new “Effective” date.
