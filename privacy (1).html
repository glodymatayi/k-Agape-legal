2) Data we collect

  • Contact: name, email, phone (may be verified by SMS).
  • Profile: gender, date of birth, country/city, denomination/church, church attendance, relationship goals, social links, and your faith narrative.
  • Uploads (optional): photos you choose to provide.
  • Selfie (optional but recommended): an image captured for manual identity verification. We do not derive biometric templates unless you later opt into an automated provider.
  • Technical/Security: device/browser info, IP-derived region; App Check tokens, reCAPTCHA scores; basic analytics.
  • Sensitive data: the faith-related information you submit. We process this only with your explicit consent for matching.

3) How we use data

  • Review applications and determine eligibility for onboarding.
  • Verify identity (e.g., SMS and optional selfie), prevent abuse, protect the community (e.g., Firebase App Check and reCAPTCHA).
  • Communicate decisions, onboarding steps, and—if opted in—updates and invitations.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the service; comply with law; enforce policies.

4) Lawful bases (GDPR, if applicable)

  • Consent: faith information; selfie capture; marketing emails; international transfers where required.
  • Contract: process your application and provide requested services.
  • Legitimate interests: community safety, fraud prevention, service improvement.
  • Legal obligation: to comply with applicable laws and lawful requests.

5) Sharing & processors

We do not sell personal data. We share information with service providers who help us run the service, including:

  • Google Firebase: Firestore, Storage, Authentication, App Check.
  • SMS via Firebase Auth (Google): phone verification.
  • Hosting/analytics providers for the website.
  • Optional, if enabled later: an identity vendor (e.g., Stripe Identity/Persona) for automated liveness/face match with separate consent.

6) Selfie capture for verification

If you choose to submit a selfie, we store the image solely for manual identity verification and community safety. We do not compute or store biometric templates unless we explicitly ask for additional consent to use an automated identity vendor. You can withdraw consent at any time by contacting us; we will delete the selfie unless we must retain it for safety, fraud prevention, or legal reasons.

7) Retention

  • Pending applications: up to 12 months.
  • Rejected applications: up to 6 months.
  • Approved/active members: while the account is active, and up to 2 years after closure for safety and legal purposes.
  • Selfies: 90 days if pending/rejected; for approved users, while account is active + 2 years.
  • Server/security logs: up to 12 months.

8) Your rights

  • Request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (e.g., selfie, faith info, marketing).
  • Data portability (where applicable).
  • Lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority (EU/UK residents).

9) Security

We apply administrative, technical, and organizational measures (e.g., App Check, least-privilege access, storage rules, monitoring). No system is 100% secure.

10) Children

K-agape is for adults 18+. If you believe a minor has provided data, contact us to remove it.

11) International transfers

Your data may be processed in countries where our providers operate (e.g., the United States). Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses and obtain your consent.

12) Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be posted with a new “Effective” date.
